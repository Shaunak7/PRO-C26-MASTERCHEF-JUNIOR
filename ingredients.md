## Ingredients Of Vegetable Momos

- **For Dough:**
- 2 cups maida
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp baking powder
- **For Filling:**
- 1 cup carrots, grated
- 1 cup cabbage, grated
- 1 tbsp oil
- 1/2 cup onion, finely chopped
- 1 tsp garlic, chopped
- 1 tsp soya sauce
- salt
- 1/4 tsp vinegar
- 1/4 tsp black pepper
